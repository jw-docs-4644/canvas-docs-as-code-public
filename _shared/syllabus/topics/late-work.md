# Late Work

I DO accept late papers (anything worth 100 points or more), BUT late work will be marked down 1 full letter grade (10 percentage points) for every day that it is late. Canvas does this automatically for every assignment. Canvas also rounds up, so one minute late is considered a full day late.

However, if there are factors outside of your control that will prevent you from turning a major paper in on time, e-mail me at least 72 hours before the deadline to request an extension. I am usually happy to negotiate a few extra days—with no late penalty. But this policy only applies if I have agreed to the extension in writing before the paper is due.
