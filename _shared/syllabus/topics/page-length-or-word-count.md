# Page Length or Word Count

Each assignment will come with a minimum word or page count. If you turn in a paper that has fewer words or pages than the minimum, then you haven’t done the minimum amount of work required for the assignment. Short assignments lose 5% of the grade for every 5% they come in under the minimum. For a page to count towards the page count, it must be entirely filled with words. The Works Cited page does not count towards the word or page count.
