# Attendance Policy for Face to Face Classes

Attendance is important to your success in this class. That said, I recognize that you have competing demands on your time. As such, the attendance policy for ENG 301 is as follows: 

- There are no “excused” absences.
- You can miss class 3 times, no questions asked. You do not need to explain your absence in any way.
- After the third absence, your final grade will be marked down a full letter grade for every day you are absent, regardless of the reason.  
- **Missing more than 6 classes will result in an automatic F for the class.** 

If you need to miss class for military service or a religious observance, let me know ahead of time, and we will work out a way for you to make up the missed class time.
