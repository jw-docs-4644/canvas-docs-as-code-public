# Revising Papers

After you've had time to revise your work based on the feedback you get on your draft, you will submit the paper for a grade. After getting that grade, you can continue to revise the paper and have it graded again, if you'd like to do so. Here's how:

1. Make your revisions.
2. Write a reflective revision memo explaining the changes you made and how those changes make the assignment more effective.
3. Submit everything to the same assignment prompt and I will re-grade it.

You can revise as many times as you like until the last week of class. After that, I can't accept any more revised work.
