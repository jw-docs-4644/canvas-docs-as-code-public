# Use of ChatGPT or other Generative AI Tools

Artificial Intelligence tools have their place in the professional writing world, and we are still learning what that place will be. For this class, it may be tempting to use AI to complete the small, regular writing assignments. I really hope you will choose not to do this, since those are "writing to learn" activities. In other words, it is through that writing that you’ll do a much of the learning I hope you’ll do in this class. If you use AI to complete writing work in the class, then your learning will be hampered. 

For larger assignments, it may be helpful to use AI tools to generate competing perspectives or even to help with outlining. But any text that you produce and turn in for credit should be written entirely by you. Papers generated by AI tools will be considered plagiarized and will result in the consequences outlined in the Scholastic Integrity section of this syllabus.
