# Paper Formatting

Unless otherwise noted in the assignment description, please follow all aspects of MLA formatting for the work you do in this class. The [Purdue Online Writing Lab](https://owl.purdue.edu/owl/research_and_citation/mla_style/mla_formatting_and_style_guide/mla_formatting_and_style_guide.html) provides a great summary of MLA style.  Here are a few questions that often come up about paper formatting.

- Font: 12 point, Times New Roman or Calibri
- Line Spacing: Double.
- Page Margins: 1 inch on all sides. (NOTE: Microsoft Word sometimes defaults to larger left and right margins. You may have to change the margins in your papers!)
- Effective use of headings is encouraged.
