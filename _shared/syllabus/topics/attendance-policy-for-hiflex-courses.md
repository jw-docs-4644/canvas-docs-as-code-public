# Attendance Policy for Hiflex Courses

Since this is a Hiflex class, you have a couple of options when it comes to attendance:

-   Attend Synchronously:
    -   Come to our regular scheduled meeting times, which are listed at the top of the syllabus and in your course schedule.
    -   If you are in Ellensburg, please try to attend in person.
    -   If you are not in Ellensburg, you can attend using the Zoom link, which is listed in Canvas.
    -   If you usually attend synchronously, but have to miss a lecture for any reason, you can attend asynchronously when needed.
-   Attend Asynchronously:
    -   You can plan on attending all of our class sessions asynchronously.
    -   Just view the recorded lecture after I post it to Canvas and write a reflection.
    -   There is a prompt for the reflections in Canvas.
    -   During some lectures I will provide additional prompts as well.
-   Lecture attendance is marked as Complete or Incomplete.

Regardless of how you choose to attend, you may miss up to three lectures without your grade being affected. After the third missed lecture, your overall class grade will be lowered by up to a letter grade per absence. If you miss six classes, you will fail the class.
