# Grading Scale
The following table contains the overall grading scale used in this class:

|Letter Grade | Range |
|-------------|-------|
|A            |100% to 94%|
|A-           |94% to 90% |
|B+           |90% to 87% |
|B            |87% to 84% |
|B-           |84% to 80% |
|C+           |80% to 77% |
|C            |77% to 74% |
|C-           |74% to 70% |
|D+           |70% to 67% |
|D            |67% to 64% |
|D-           |64% to 61% |
|F            |61% to 0%  |

At the end of the quarter, Canvas calculates the percentage of points you have earned in this class.  I do not round up when figuring your final grade. If at any time, you think anything is amiss in the grade book, please email me and give me a chance to correct it!
